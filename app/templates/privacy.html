Privacy Policy

Overview

At Pollify, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our polling service.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Poll questions and options you create
  • Vote responses
  • Browser cookies to remember your polls

Cookie Usage

We use cookies to:

  • Remember polls you've created
  • Provide quick access to your poll management
  • Improve your user experience

Our cookies are stored locally in your browser for 30 days.

Data Storage

Poll data is stored in our secure database. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track individual voters
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use data for advertising

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your polls at any time
  • Clear your browser cookies
  • Request information about your data

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at info@urbanmechanics.co.uk
